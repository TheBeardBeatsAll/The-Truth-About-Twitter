What is the purpose of this site?

With the advent of social media there has been a rise of several types of fake accounts across each platform. They range from benign automated company accounts to more sinister accounts masquerading as real humans. This website allows you to see if a Twitter account of their choosing is likely to be a real person or a fake account.

How does this site tell the difference?

Datasets from the Bot Repository were used to train machine learning models to predict between real, fake follower, traditional spambot and social spambot account types with some of the elements that are looked at for these models are if there are any links in an accounts description, the ratio of friends to followers, amount of times tweeted, the use of the default profile image and more.

DISCLAIMER

Predictions are not 100% accurate and will be wrong from time to time especially when an odd real account or newer forms of bot accounts are encountered.
