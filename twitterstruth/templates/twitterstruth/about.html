The Purpose of this site

Allowing anyone to input a Twitter account and whether it is predicted to be a real person or a form of fake or bot account. This is important because Twitter and other social media platforms have become polluted with fake accounts. Datasets from the Bot Repository were used to train machine learning models to make these predictions based off of certain account properties.

Type of Predictions for an Account

Real - A real person
Real (Verified) - Verified by Twitter to be who they say they are
Fake Follower - A type of bot account that boosts other accounts follower numbers
Traditional Bot - A type of bot account that masquerades somewhat OK as human
Social Bot - A type of bot account that masquerades effectively as human

DISCLAIMER: Predictions are not 100% accurate
